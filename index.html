About Me

They call me
Will Su

Hi all! I'm a rising sophomore student at Rice, majoring in Computer Science. This summer, I lived in Shanghai and worked as a Data Science intern at Lam Research Corporation. I've been growing more curious about the multitude of means Data Science can benefit society everyday throughout my intern, but at the same time, I've also found Web Design to be more interesting than I previously imagined since I started creating this website.

In my free time, you can probably find me in the nearest gym playing pickup games or just shooting the basketball. I also love traveling around the world and have been to 10 countries so far. Everytime I travel, I try to capture snapshots of each city's beauty through my camera's lens. If you're interested in my work, check out my photography on Instagram that's linked below. (Find my LinkedIn and there as well!) I'm also a lowercase advocate. It basically means I think lowercase letters are prettier than uppercases for no good reason. If you see me type with uppercases, it's probably my attempt at being professional.
